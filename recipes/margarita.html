Strawberry margarita

A near perfect strawberry margarita with frozen strawberries and limeade concentrate.

  • 6 fluid ounces tequila
  • 2 fluid ounces triple sec
  • 8 ounces frozen sliced strawberries in syrup
  • 4 fluid ounces frozen limeade concentrate
  1. Fill a blender with ice and crush. Pour in the tequila and triple sec. Add the strawberries and limeade.

  2. Blend for 30 seconds or until smooth.

  3. Serve in margarita glasses with the rims dipped in powdered sugar.
